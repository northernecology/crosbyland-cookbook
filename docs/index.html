1.1 Pickles

1.1.1 Rhubarb pickles

On May 25, 2022, I tried my first batch of pickled rhubarb (we have a LOT of rhubarb from only 2 plants in the backyard). The recipe comes from phickle.com

Ingredients:

  • 7 rhubarb stalks, cut in 1 inch pieces
  • 1 big garlic clove, minced
  • 1 Tbsp black peppercorns
  • 1.5 tsp creamed horse radish root
  • 2.5% brine solution: 425 grams of water mixed with 10 grams of pickling salt

I put the garlic, peppercorns, and horse radish into the bottom of the jar, and then the rhubarb pieces on top. The rhubarb came almost to the top of the jar.

I made the brine with room temperature bottled water from Costco mixed with Rogers pickling salt, and stirred until the salt was dissolved.

I poured the brine over the rhubarb and it was just enough to cover everything. I put a half-pint jar on top of everything to keep the vegetables submerged, and secured it all by putting a piece of cheese cloth over the top and securing it to the big jar with a rubber band.

Now I will wait 2 weeks and see what I get.
